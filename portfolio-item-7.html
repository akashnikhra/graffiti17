    NAUTANKI S[H]AALA



    Register Now
    Prizes Worth ₹ 50,000

    Event Details

    STAND UP COMEDY(The tickle trail)

    Team size: solo/duet

    Date: 22nd January, 2017

    Registration fees: ₹100

    Rules

    • It is either a solo or a duet competition.
    • Registrations will be accepted on first come first serve basis and prior to the event.
    • There will be one round and result will be declared after all performances.
    • The maximum duration for performance is 4+2 minutes.
    • Negative marking if performance exceeds 6 minutes.
    • Participants will be judged on the basis of content, fluency, spontaneity, presentation & sense of humor.
    • Language restricted to Hindi, Marathi and English.
    • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
    • Script of the act must be original.
    • Caution should be taken to refrain from displaying obscenity, violence, prejudice, defamation towards any individual, community or religion.
    STREET PLAY(Nukkad natak)

    Team size: 8-18

    Date: 21st January, 2017

    Registration fees: ₹300

    Rules

    • A team should have a minimum of 8 and a maximum of 18 people (exclusive of all helpers).
    • There will be one round and result will be declared after all performances.
    • The maximum duration for performance is 15+3 minutes, including set-up time.
    • Team shall attract negative marking after 18 minutes.
    • No electronic music instruments and sound system or mikes will be allowed.
    • Uniformity in dress will be appreciated.
    • Plays with social relevance are expected.
    • Use of fire (even Diyas, candles or lighters) and water in the act is not allowed.
    • Language of play (is/should be) restricted to Hindi, Marathi and English.
    • Teams have to bring their own props and costumes.
    • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
    • Script of the street play must be original.
    • Caution should be taken to refrain from displaying obscenity, violence, prejudice, defamation towards any individual, community or religion.
    MIME

    Team size: 3-8

    Date: 22nd January, 2017

    Registration fees: ₹100

    Rules

    • It is a team event. Each team should have 3-8 members only (exclusive of all helpers).
    • The act should not contain any dialogues, lip sync, vocal sound.
    • Teams must bring their own background music for mime, if any.
    • Time limit for performing the mime is 8+2 minutes.
    • Team shall attract negative marking after 10 minutes.
    • Caution should be taken to refrain from displaying obscenity, violence, prejudice, defamation towards any individual, community or religion.
    • Teams have to bring their own make up (white grease paint, eye/lip liners), props and costumes.
    • There will be one round and result will be declared after all performances.
    • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
