    Rules

    • Participants are required to bring their College ID Cards and Registration receipts.
    • Teams will have to strictly adhere to reporting time for every round, failing which they would be disqualified.
    • Details of each round will be disclosed at the time of the event.
    • All Rounds will be eliminatory in nature.
    • Participants will have to carry watches and manage their time. Volunteers will not assist them in keeping time for any round, though the teams will be monitored for the same.
    • Participants are not allowed to communicate/seek help from anyone other than their own team members.
    • Any damage to property within the SIC campus will lead to immediate disqualification of the team from the game and penalized monetarily.
    • Use of cell phones or any other communication/electronic device is prohibited unless specified by Team Graffiti. Usage of the same will lead to disqualification.
    • In case of any changes or discrepancies, the decision of Team Graffiti would be final and binding.
