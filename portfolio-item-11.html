    Biz-Launch (B-Plan)

    give wings to your dream


    Register Now

    Event Details

    Team size: 4

    Date: 21st January, 2017

    Registration fees: ₹ 150

    Prize worth: ₹ 12000

    Rules

    • This event consists of 2 rounds.
    • First round is an online round and is elimination round. The shortlisted team would be intimated through mail and the final round would take place at campus.
    • For the first round, Each team has to mail their business plan ( format and other details related to the proposal would be intimated through mail later).
    • Participants will have to submit and present their start-up idea with the following parameters:
      1. Identification of business opportunity
      2. Developing a business model
      3. The three C’s of business( company, competitor, customer)
      4. Financials of the business such as :
        • Source of funds
        • Fund allocation on assets
        • Revenue model
        • Breakeven(profit making)
        • Plan to repayment loan- if any
        • Contingency plan
        • Expenses
        • Insurance
        • Legal forms of business
    • Participating teams are expected to submit original ideas. Plagiarized or copied ideas will lead to disqualification.
    • The shortlisted team may or may not use PPT for presentation for the final round and the time limit for the presentation will be communicated through mailer.
    • The participating team members should carry their College ID Card and registration receipt.
    • Use of cell phones or any other communication device is not allowed during the event
    • In case of any changes or discrepancies, the decision of event coordinators would be final and binding.
